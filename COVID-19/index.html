Symptoms of COVID-19

• Dry cough, cold, sore throat
• Fever or chills
• Fatigue, tiredness, muscle or body aches
• Headache
• Congestion or runny nose
• Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
• Loss of appetite/taste/smell
